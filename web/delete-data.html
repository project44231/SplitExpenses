Delete Your Data

We respect your right to control your personal information. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from SplitPot, please follow the instructions below.

What Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
  • All game data and history
  • Player contacts and information you've added
  • Buy-in records and settlements
  • All statistics and leaderboards
  • Any uploaded images or files

How to Delete Your Data

Follow these steps to request data deletion:

  1. Send an email to project44231@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information in your email:
    • The email address associated with your account
    • Your name (as shown in the app)
    • Confirmation that you want all data permanently deleted
  4. We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours
  5. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Alternative: Delete Data from the App

You can also delete your data directly from the app:

  1. Open the SplitPot app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision

Note: In-app deletion is immediate and permanent.

Guest Mode Users

If you used the app in Guest Mode (without signing in):

  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • Simply uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Guest data on our servers is automatically cleaned up periodically

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or our privacy practices, please contact us at project44231@gmail.com.
