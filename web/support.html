Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

Tap "Sign in with Google" on the welcome screen. You can also use Guest Mode to try the app without signing in.

How do I start a game?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select players, choose your currency, and tap "Start Game".

Can I edit buy-ins after adding them?

Yes! Tap on a player card to expand it, then tap the edit icon on any buy-in to modify or delete it.

How do settlements work?

After entering cash-outs, SplitPot automatically calculates optimized settlements that minimize the number of transactions needed.

How do I share live game standings?

Sign in, start a game, then tap the share icon in the top right. Copy the link and share it with anyone.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app works offline. Your data will sync automatically when you're back online (if signed in).

How do I delete my account?

Visit our Data Deletion page or email us at project44231@gmail.com with your request.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely with Firebase. See our Privacy Policy for details.
